Our Story

Worn Well

🦭

This space is for women in seasons where their bodies, time, and energy are poured into others.

🌊

Mother seals lose a significant amount of weight and nutrients as they give their everything to their cubs. Like seals, mothers change — not because they are failing — but because they are giving.

🌿

The name Worn Well honors that truth. To be worn is not to be broken. It is a mark of love given freely, of presence offered fully, of a season lived with your whole self.

🐚

This is a place to rest, refill, and remember who you are — while your little ones play safely nearby. You don't have to leave to find yourself. You just have to stop long enough to breathe.

Come as you are.You are already enough.

Questions & Answers

We've Got Answers

Everything you need to know before your first visit.

Does my child need anything specific to play? +
Just a pair of socks! All children (and adults) must wear socks in the play area — no shoes allowed inside the space. We recommend bringing a spare pair just in case. Everything else is provided — we keep our toys clean, our floors padded, and our space stocked with all the good stuff.
What ages are allowed in the play room? +
The play space is designed for children ages 6 months through 7 years. We've thoughtfully curated the environment to be safe and stimulating for babies, toddlers, and big kids alike — with separate areas for crawlers and walkers.
Do I need to reserve a time to play? +
For our soft launch (March 23–28), yes — reservations are required so we can make sure every family gets the space and attention they deserve. Each session is 90 minutes and limited to 5 families. After launch, members will have flexible drop-in options available.
Can I come in just for a drink? +
Absolutely! The café is open to everyone — no admission needed. Admission fees only apply when you'd like to use the play space. So if you want to pop in, grab a Hibiscus Tide, and take a breath — you're more than welcome anytime.
What is the admission fee and what does it include? +
Admission is $10 for your first child and $8 for each additional child. This covers your full 90-minute session in the play space. Add any food item from our menu and receive $2 off your admission. Members enjoy discounted or included admission depending on their plan.
What if my child is sick? +
We kindly ask that you stay home if your child is showing any signs of illness — fever, runny nose, cough, vomiting, or anything contagious. We want to keep our space safe and healthy for all of our littles and their mamas. We'll always work with you to reschedule.
Can I bring food for my child? +
Outside food and drinks for children are welcome, but must stay in the café seating area — not inside the play space. This helps us keep the floors clean and safe for all the crawlers and rollers in the room. We do have a kids' snack menu if you'd like to order something while you're here!
How does membership work? +
We offer three tiers: Drop-In ($26) for single visits, Monthly ($55/month) for unlimited visits and priority booking, and Village ($99/month) which includes everything in Monthly plus private event access, a monthly mama gathering, and 20% off all menu items. You can view full details on the Membership page.
Is Worn Well only for moms? +
Worn Well is a space designed with mothers in mind, but all caregivers are warmly welcome — dads, grandparents, aunts, nannies, and anyone who shows up for a little one. Our community events are primarily mama-focused, but the café and play space are open to all loving caregivers.

Still have a question? We'd love to hear from you.
